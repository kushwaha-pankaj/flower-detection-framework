## Tips for Efficient Searching

### arXiv
1. Use advanced search: `(meta-learning OR few-shot) AND (crop OR flower OR agriculture)`
2. Filter by category on left: cs.CV, cs.LG, cs.RO
3. Sort by relevance, not date
4. Check "cited by" links to find related work
5. Download papers as you go; batch read later

### IEEE Xplore
1. Use quotes for exact phrases: `"robotic pollination"`
2. Combine multiple AND clauses
3. Filter for peer-reviewed content (exclude standards, early access if possible)
4. Check author profiles for other papers
5. Note which conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ICRA are top-tier)

### ResearchGate
1. Search researcher profiles working on related topics
2. Browse their publications
3. Check "Related Research" suggestions
4. Some researchers respond to direct questions
5. Note: Predatory publishers exist on RG; verify venues
