## General Research Databases & Access Points

### Primary Academic Databases:
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/ - Free access to many papers
- **Nature Scientific Reports**: https://www.nature.com/srep/ - Open access journal
- **Frontiers in Plant Science**: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/plant-science - Open access
- **MDPI Journals**: https://www.mdpi.com/ - Open access multidisciplinary publisher
- **IEEE Xplore**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/ - IEEE conference and journal papers
- **ResearchGate**: https://www.researchgate.net/ - Many researchers share PDFs

### Alternative Access:
- **arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/ - Preprints (check for computer vision/robotics papers)
- **Springer Link**: https://link.springer.com/ - Journal articles with DOI links
- **ASME Digital Collection**: https://asmedigitalcollection.asme.org/ - ASME conference papers
- **Institutional Repositories**: University websites often host faculty publications

### If You Don't Have Access:
1. **Contact the Authors** - Researchers often share papers via ResearchGate or email
2. **Use Sci-Hub** (if available in your region) - https://sci-hub.se/
3. **Check Your University Library** - Many universities provide database access
4. **Use a VPN** - Some universities allow remote access to subscriptions
5. **Request from Digital Libraries** - LibGen or open library services
